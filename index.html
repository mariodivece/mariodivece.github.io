About Me

Hello, I am Mario Di Vece, a Mexican Entrepreneur and Electrical Engineer. I graduated from the University of Alberta in 2006. I have worked for almost 20 years in the development of software, artificial intelligence projects, computer vision, multimedia, automation, and embedded systems. I have extensive experience in the real estate, petroleum, automotive, financial services, and health services industries in domestic and foreign markets.

I like cars, playing tennis, dogs, and (to a lesser degree) cats. You can visit my LinkedIn page here. It's the best way you can contact me.

My main goal and mission in life focuses on the development and enlargement of the middle class. I will continue to create and grow businesses that encourage and facilitate professional, economic, and social development for all my employees and their families.
